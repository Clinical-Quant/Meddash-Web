## Findings

### Thread: [KOL Mapping Software - Is it worth it?](https://www.reddit.com/r/MedicalScienceLiaison/comments/1i18rkw/kol_mapping_software_is_it_worth_it/) (r/MedicalScienceLiaison)
- **Pain Points:** 
    - Accuracy: Users report ~25% of mapping data is erroneous.
    - Redundancy: 75% of the data becomes obvious to an MSL within 3-4 months of active territory management.
    - Role: Often viewed as a "side tool" rather than a primary source.
- **Pricing/Value:** 
    - Described as "silly expensive".
    - Many MSLs find it "useless fairly quickly" relative to the cost.
- **Feature Requests/Utilities:**
    - Better tracking of site PIs for competitor studies and publications.
    - Claims data (available in Monocl via subscription) is noted as potentially helpful.
    - Identifying background details *after* identification is a common use case for Monocl.
- **Alternative Methods:**
    - Manual research, professional networks, commercial team relationships, medical societies, and publication searches remain the primary drivers.

### Thread: [Those of you without Monocl, what is your strategy for identifying KOL email addresses?](https://www.reddit.com/r/MedicalScienceLiaison/comments/1evjayp/those_of_you_without_monocl_what_is_your_strategy/) (r/MedicalScienceLiaison)
- **Pain Points:** 
    - Budget Cuts: High-cost tools like Monocl are often the first to be cut, leaving MSLs with manual work.
    - Outreach Friction: Finding accurate KOL email addresses is extremely time-consuming ("many many hours").
    - Data Decay: High bounce rates with tools like Rocketreach or manual guessing.
- **Alternatives & Workarounds:**
    - **Apollo:** Free version and paid ($50/mo+) are mentioned as effective alternatives with good LinkedIn integration.
    - **Medical Societies:** ASCO member directory (Oncology) is a key resource.
    - **Creative Guessing:** Using institution-specific email formats found via other authors' emails on PubMed.
    - **HCP License Registries:** Some state registries list emails (though often front desk).
- **Wish List:** 
    - Direct, verified email access integrated with identification tools.
    - Integration with social/professional platforms like LinkedIn.
