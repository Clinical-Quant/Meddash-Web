## Findings
- **H1 Insights / HCP Universe (Enterprise Solution):**
    - **Deliverables:** Over 250 attributes per HCP profile.
        - **Demographics:** Name, affiliations (primary/secondary), education, contact info.
        - **Clinical/Expertise:** Clinical specialty, research interests, conditions/indications.
        - **Academic Activity:** Publications (full list), congresses (posters, speaking engagements), clinical trials (PI status, site locations).
        - **Commercial/Claims:** Global claims data (ICD-10/procedure codes), treatment volumes, patient counts.
        - **Industry Payments:** Sunshine Act/Open Payments data.
        - **Social/Digital:** "Digital Opinion Leader" (DOL) tracking, social media reach, and engagement.
    - **Features:**
        - **Vida:** Generative AI assistant for quick insights (e.g., "Find top KOLs in oncology in the UK").
        - **H1 Trial Landscape:** Module for clinical trial site selection and recruitment.
        - **Territory Management:** MSL planning and engagement tracking.
    - **Data Coverage:** 10M+ HCPs, 10B+ global claims, 420k+ clinical trials, 150+ countries.
- **H1 Connect (HCP Solution):**
    - Free community for HCPs (formerly Faculty Opinions).
    - Features: Expert commentary on research, personalized paper recommendations, impact factor tracking.
    - Acts as a top-of-funnel for H1's data ecosystem.
- **Pricing:** Not public. Typically enterprise-level deals ($50k-$500k range based on company size and seats). No "self-serve" pricing available.
- **Customer Sentiment:** Generally positive for data breadth, but some notes on UI complexity and the need for high-quality data cleaning (common in large-scale claims data).
