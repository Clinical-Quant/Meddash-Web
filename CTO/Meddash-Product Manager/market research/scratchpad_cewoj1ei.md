# Meddash Product Manager - IQVIA Competitor Research

## Findings so far:
- **Product Names:** 
    - **Expert Engagement Platform / Expert Ecosystem:** Main platform for KOL identification and mapping.
    - **OneKey:** The foundational database for 25M+ HCPs and 6M organizations.
    - **Digital Expert:** Module for Digital Opinion Leaders (DOLs).
    - **Share of Scientific Voice (SoSV™):** Proprietary metric for influence.
- **Data Sources:** 
    - OneKey: 25 million HCPs, 118+ countries, 1.5 million monthly updates.
    - 1,000+ data attributes per profile (license, address, specialty, etc.).
    - 1.7 Billion medical claims (US/Global data).
    - 43k+ scientific meetings, publications, trials, and social media.
- **Included Info in Profiles:** 
    - Professional affiliations (hospitals, clinics, IDNs).
    - Scientific impact (publications, citations, trials).
    - Digital footprint (DOL status, social reach).
    - Network connectivity (referral patterns, peer connections).
    - Share of Voice in specific TAs or meetings.
- **Pricing:** 
    - **Enterprise SaaS:** $50k-$200k+ annual subscription (est. based on G-Cloud bench).
    - **Service/Study:** Likely $20k-$100k per mapping project depending on KOL count.
- **Timelines:** 
    - **SaaS Access:** Immediate (database is live).
    - **Custom Mapping Study:** 8 to 12 weeks for a thorough global/regional project.
- **Deliverables:** 
    - User-facing dashboards (Expert Ecosystem).
    - Custom excel/tiered lists.
    - Social/Digital network visualizations.
