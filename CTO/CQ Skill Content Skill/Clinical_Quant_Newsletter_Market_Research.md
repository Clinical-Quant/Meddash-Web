## 4. CONTENT MODEL & STYLE GUIDE

### Article Structure (3-Minute Read)
```
[HEADLINE] [TICKER] Event Type | One-line summary

📰 THE EVENT (30 sec)
- What happened: factual statement
- Source: SEC/FDA/CT.gov citation
- Date: specific timestamp

🔍 HORIZONTAL CONTEXT (60 sec)
- Company background: stage, cash runway
- Previous catalysts: historical pattern
- Competitive landscape: similar drugs/trials

💡 KEY NUGGET (30 sec)
- One actionable insight
- What to watch next

➡️ WHAT'S NEXT (20 sec)
- Upcoming catalysts
- FDA dates to watch

[DISCLAIMER]
[LINK TO FULL ANALYSIS - paywalled]
```

### Style Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- ✅ **Objective language:** "ABC Pharma share price closed 3.5%..."
- ❌ **No hype:** "ABC Pharma has performed bad" (judgmental)
- ✅ **Temperature 0.0** - No speculation without data
- ✅ **Tickers first** in headlines for SEO
- ✅ **Executive tone** - Simple, professional, no jargon
- ✅ **One valuable nugget** per article guaranteed

### Sample Headline Formats
- "[MDBX] CEO Acquires $5M via Form 4 | Insider Signal Pre-PDUFA"
- "[ABC] FDA PDUFA Date Announced: June 15 | NDA for X Disease"
- "[XYZ] Phase 2 Topline: Primary Endpoint Missed | Next Steps?"
